About the South Cornwall Telegraph Pole Society

Dedicated members of the South Cornwall Telegraph Pole Society, united by their passion for preserving the region's telegraph pole heritage.

Discover the Legacy and Future of Telegraph Poles in South Cornwall

Who We Are

The South Cornwall Telegraph Pole Society is a dedicated group of enthusiasts, engineers, historians, and nature lovers who share a passion for the iconic telegraph poles that dot the landscape of Cornwall. Our mission is to celebrate, preserve, and promote the historical, cultural, and engineering significance of these fascinating structures.

Our Mission

We are committed to preserving the heritage of telegraph poles in South Cornwall while fostering a community of like-minded individuals who appreciate their role in shaping communication across rural Britain. Through educational events, preservation projects, and regular meetups, we aim to increase awareness of the value these poles hold, not just in telecommunications, but in the history and beauty of Cornwall.

What We Do

  • Preservation and Restoration: We focus on the restoration of historic poles, ensuring they remain part of the local landscape for future generations to appreciate.
  • Education: Our workshops, seminars, and educational resources cover a wide range of topics, from the history of telegraphy to the art of pole maintenance.
  • Engagement: Through walks, events, and projects, we bring together enthusiasts and volunteers to enjoy and learn more about Cornwall's telegraph poles.
  • Documentation and Research: We maintain a growing archive of historical records and photographs, documenting the evolution of telegraph poles in the region.

Our Story

Founded in 2003, the South Cornwall Telegraph Pole Society was born out of a shared appreciation for these utilitarian yet often overlooked structures. What began as a small group of enthusiasts has now grown into a vibrant community dedicated to preserving the past while exploring the future of telegraph poles. From the historic poles standing tall along the cliffs of Land’s End to the modern composite poles of St. Austell, we embrace the diversity and importance of all telegraph poles in the region.

Join Us

Whether you’re a lifelong Cornwall resident, a recent visitor, or someone with a growing interest in telegraphy, we welcome you to join our Society. With regular events, fascinating talks, and plenty of opportunities to get involved, there’s always something happening. Become a part of our community and help us ensure that the stories of South Cornwall’s telegraph poles are never forgotten.
