Events

Celebrate the Legacy of Telegraph Poles in South Cornwall

Join us throughout the year for a variety of events celebrating the history, preservation, and beauty of telegraph poles. From guided walks and hands-on workshops to exhibitions and community clean-ups, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Explore our calendar of events and be a part of preserving Cornwall's unique telegraph pole heritage!

Telegraph Pole History and Heritage Walk

Date: Saturday, February 17, 2024

Location: Starting at St. Austell Train Station, Cornwall

Description: A guided walking tour of South Cornwall’s historic telegraph poles, exploring their role in Cornwall’s communication history.

Annual Telegraph Pole Festival

Date: Saturday, May 5, 2024

Location: Porthcawl Recreation Ground, St. Austell

Description: A day full of workshops, talks, art exhibitions, and displays to celebrate Cornwall’s iconic telegraph poles.

Pole Painting and Restoration Workshop

Date: Saturday, June 2, 2024

Location: Fowey Heritage Centre, Fowey

Description: A hands-on workshop where members learn how to restore and preserve telegraph poles, with guidance from experts in the field.

Meet the Engineers: Behind the Scenes of Pole Construction

Date: Thursday, July 12, 2024

Location: Truro Engineering Works, Truro

Description: A special event where pole engineers reveal the technology and techniques behind building and installing telegraph poles.

The Telegraph Pole Art Exhibition

Date: August 10-24, 2024

Location: The Old Library, St. Ives

Description: An exhibition featuring local artists’ works inspired by Cornwall’s telegraph poles. The show will include photography, paintings, and sculptures.

Night Sky Photography and Telegraph Pole Viewing

Date: Saturday, September 15, 2024

Location: Lizard Point, Cornwall

Description: Capture stunning photos of telegraph poles with the night sky as your backdrop, followed by a discussion on the history and significance of these structures.
