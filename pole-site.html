South Cornwall Telegraph Pole Society

Timeless Connections: Telegraph poles weaving through the serene landscapes of South Cornwall.

Welcome to the fasinating and engaging world of telegraph poles in the south cornwall region

Greetings and a warm welcome to all members and enthusiasts, new and seasoned, of the South Cornwall Telegraph Pole Society! Whether you’ve joined us out of a deep appreciation for the artistry of utility poles, a fascination with their engineering marvels, or sheer curiosity, you’ve found a vibrant and welcoming community.

Nestled in one of the most picturesque corners of the United Kingdom, South Cornwall offers more than just rolling hills, rugged coastlines, and charming villages—it’s also home to a fascinating network of telegraph poles. These silent sentinels have stood watch over our landscapes for generations, connecting communities and preserving the legacy of communication. From weathered, timeworn poles that carry a sense of history to the freshly erected modern structures heralding technological progress, these poles represent a fusion of heritage and innovation.

As a society, we view these structures not just as tools of utility but as cultural and historical artifacts. Telegraph poles have played a pivotal role in the evolution of communication, and each one tells its own unique story of craftsmanship, resilience, and connection. They remind us of the human ingenuity that made long-distance communication possible and the countless hands that worked tirelessly to build and maintain these networks.

Here at the South Cornwall Telegraph Pole Society, we celebrate these icons in every possible way. We organize guided walks to explore the most historic and picturesque poles in the region, giving members a chance to learn about their history while soaking in Cornwall’s breathtaking scenery. Our hands-on workshops dive deep into the craftsmanship and maintenance of poles, offering a rare glimpse into the art of telegraphy preservation. For those who love to capture beauty through a lens, our photography contests highlight the poles’ aesthetic charm as they weave through the Cornish countryside.

But our community is about more than just events and activities—it’s about the connections we build with one another. We are a society that thrives on shared passion, collaboration, and the joy of discovering something extraordinary in the ordinary. Whether you're here to explore the rich history of telecommunication, marvel at the elegance of telegraph poles, or simply connect with like-minded individuals, we are delighted to have you join us.

Take some time to browse our website, where you’ll find details of upcoming events, insightful articles, and opportunities to get involved in preservation projects. If you’re brimming with ideas, stories, or even photographs that you’d like to share, don’t hesitate to reach out—we’d love to hear from you!

Together, let’s ensure that the spirit of telegraph pole appreciation remains alive and thriving in South Cornwall for generations to come. Welcome aboard, and we look forward to sharing this journey with you!
